The Periodic Task Scheduler

Some of our recurring tasks occur on a specific day, and our existing calendar software can easily keep track of them. Things like "Taxes due" or "Pay rent" can be set to a specific day of the month or year, and if you miss the date it doesn't matter because it's coming back around on that same day again next time.

But many times we wish to keep track of things we need to do regularly, but don't care if it is linked to a particular calendar date. And if we complete the task early or late, we want to be reminded again relative to when we actually completed it, not when we should have completed it.

Cyclence is designed to keep track of exactly these kinds of tasks, when you last did them, when you should do them next, and how overdue a task may be.

Examples:

  • Change your oil
  • Schedule a checkup with the doctor
  • Clean out the fridge
  • Call your parents

Try it out. It's free and will take a massive load off your mind.
